# EPA Water Treatment Corpus

Public EPA documents selected for structural variety and domain relevance. Ozone disinfection documents included to leverage domain expertise in ozone water treatment systems.

Run `bash scripts/download_data.sh` to download all documents.

| # | File | Title | Structure |
|---|------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | `01-understanding-sdwa.pdf` | Understanding the Safe Drinking Water Act | Narrative, bullets |
| 2 | `02-npdwr-complete-table.pdf` | National Primary Drinking Water Regulations | Heavy tables |
| 3 | `03-pfas-treatment-options.pdf` | PFAS NPDWR: Treatment Options | Tables + narrative |
| 4 | `04-pfas-bat-ssct.pdf` | PFAS Best Available Technology & SSCT | Dense regulatory + tables |
| 5 | `05-dbpr-plain-english-guide.pdf` | Disinfectants & DBP Rules Plain English Guide | Headers, diagrams, tables |
| 6 | `06-disinfection-profiling-benchmarking.pdf` | Disinfection Profiling & Benchmarking Guidance | Dense technical, formulas, CT values |
| 7 | `07-ozone-disinfection-factsheet.pdf` | Ozone Disinfection Fact Sheet | Short, ozone-specific |
| 8 | `08-chemistry-ozone-disinfection.pdf` | Chemistry of Ozone Disinfection | Technical chemistry |
| 9 | `09-package-plants-factsheet.pdf` | Package Plants Fact Sheet | Sections, diagrams |
| 10 | `10-sbr-factsheet.pdf` | Sequencing Batch Reactors Fact Sheet | Sections, parameters |
| 11 | `11-emerging-technologies-wastewater.pdf` | Emerging Technologies for Wastewater Treatment | Long, tables, assessments |
| 12 | `12-wqs-handbook-ch3-criteria.pdf` | WQS Handbook Ch.3: Water Quality Criteria | Dense regulatory, legal refs |
